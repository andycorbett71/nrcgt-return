Before you start

Do not use this service if you sold the property on or after 6 April 2020. Use your Capital Gains Tax on UK property account instead.

Do not use this service if you're a company or a corporate entity. Use your HMRC online account to sign in for Corporation Tax instead.

If more than one disposal has the same completion date, complete a single return for all of them. Otherwise complete a separate return for each disposal and any changes.

For jointly held property or land, each person must tell us about their own share of the disposal.

You cannot register for this service if you’re in the UK illegally.
